Meteor 350

The Meteor 350 features a semi-digital instrument cluster with Tripper Navigation (turn-by-turn assist) TFT pod as an optional accessory. On the main pod, the speed is shown by an analogue needle with a digital display showing tripmeter, odometer, gear position, fuel level, service indicator, Eco prompt and a clock. There’s also a USB port on the left switchgear for charging on the go. There’s a dedicated hazard lamp switch on the right switchgear too. As far as safety is concerned, the motorcycle comes with dual-channel ABS as standard.
